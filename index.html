Inside the School

The Large Hall Expanding Across the School

This is the place where many students travel across the school.

This hall is very long and leads to the Pod Areas, the Cafeteria, and has the stairs that lead to the exploratory classes. The hall leads to the Gym, Band and Orchestra classes, and the exit to the outside gym areas.

The Locker Areas

Hall Locker areas

This hall shows the lockers, this is where the students go to their lockers to get material for their classes, these areas are mostly next to the Pod Areas where their classes are held.
